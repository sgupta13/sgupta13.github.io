I opted for Computer Science and Engineering as my field of study.

SBSCET served as a good platform in giving direction to my interests in Computer Science. Right from the initial days of my 1st year in the college, I got involved in learning the basics of Computer Science. Also, I used to guide my classmates in college in learning C++ as a programming language.

During my 2nd year, I got very good exposure on programming languages as well as electronic circuitry and microprocessors. At the end of my 2nd year, I did an industrial training of 6 weeks at STEP, IIT-Roorkee on J2EE and got very good technical learning there.

My 3rd year introduced me to very interesting subjects like ATM networks and Real time systems. I found these very interesting.

My philosophy that everything can be improved and the realization of the wide application of the learning can improve the things around us system strengthened my passion in the field.

During the final year at college I developed network packet sniffer along with my classmates as major project, we learn more when we apply our learning. Also, I did my 6 months industrial training developing Java projects at CMC Limited, A Tata enterprise at Noida, India.

Intuition and extensive self-introspection, especially during the practical trainings, helped me to convert my ideas into more tangible and applicable results. Curriculum courses definitely served as a good source of knowledge. I enjoyed the company of my teachers with whom I was able to learn so much. It was deeply fulfilling for me.
